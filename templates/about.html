Cloud Skills Boost Helper

Is a tool designed to help learners and professionals enhance their cloud skills by providing curated paths, courses, and labs from Google Cloud Skills Boost.

This app is built with the aim of simplifying the learning process and making it easier to track your progress in mastering cloud technologies.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced cloud professional, this app provides a structured approach to learning and mastering Google Cloud technologies.

The app is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive, allowing you to focus on learning rather than navigating complex interfaces.

Purpose

The purpose of this app is to:

  1. Provide easy access to Google Cloud learning resources.
  2. Organize paths, courses, and labs into a structured format.
  3. Track your progress and help you achieve your cloud learning goals.

Features

  1. Browse curated learning paths, courses, and labs.
  2. Search for specific topics or skills.
  3. Track your progress and revisit completed resources.
