About Cloud Skills Boost Helper

Cloud Skills Boost Helper is a tool designed to help learners and professionals enhance their cloud skills by providing curated paths, courses, and labs from Google Cloud Skills Boost. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced user, this app helps you navigate and organize your learning journey effectively.

Purpose

The purpose of this app is to:

  • Provide easy access to Google Cloud learning resources.
  • Organize paths, courses, and labs into a structured format.
  • Track your progress and help you achieve your cloud learning goals.

Features

  • Browse curated learning paths, courses, and labs.
  • Search for specific topics or skills.
  • Track your progress and revisit completed resources.
